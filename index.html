The Random Book Club

Welcome to the random book club; a small, but life-long project to collect ideas from the stacks of books I have accumulated over the years.

This site was originally created as a reference for myself and as a way to help recover from an injury that harmed my short-term memory (since I suddenly could not trust my ability to recall information correctly). Each book listed below has the specific edition I have read and every passage has the orginal page number it was found on to help with referencing.
